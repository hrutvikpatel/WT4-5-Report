APPLYBOARD

ApplyBoard is an online platform that empowers students from around the world to access the best education by simplifying the study abroad search, application and accceptance process. They have accomplished this by connecting international students, recruitment partners, and academic institutions on one platform. Applybord was founded in 2015 by brothers Martin, Meti, and Massi Basiri. They have build partnerships with over 1200 educational institutions and worked with 4000+ recruitment partners, to drive diversity on campuses across Canada, the United States, and the United Kingdom. ApplyBoard has become the world's largest online platform for recruiting interntional students. JOB DESCRIPTION

I was hired to be a part of the Platform team at ApplyBoard, as a Software Engineer for 8 months. I primarily worked on React, Rails, and our new authentication system. My day-to-day activities involved working on new feature development, bug fixing, code review, and testing. This type of work exercised my attention to detail, communication, and problem-solving skills as it involved me to diagnose, plan, and communicate with the team to develop features or resolve bugs.

Term Focus

I am working towards learning about Full Stack Development. This work term has helped me accomplish many of the skills that I require to become a Full Stack Developer, I have had to opportunity to work on React (frontend), Rails (backend framework) and solving complex integration bugs that are crucial to the Sales Team's day-to-day tasks. From this work term, I was able to gain more insight into a backend monolith architecture and what the pros and cons of using Rails are. I will take the skills that I have attained from ApplyBoard and utilize them during my academic studies.

The focus of the work term report was backend development and using tools such as Kibana to solve complex production bugs. From my mentors at ApplyBoard, I have learned that a good software developer is one that can diagnose the root cause(s) of a bug, come up with multiple solutions and then choose the most appropriate solution by looking at the pros and cons. In doing so I was able to solve 80% of the major integration bugs that ApplyBoard was facing with Hubspot which helped reduce the time it took the Sales Team to get accurate and up to date information on Hubspot from ApplyBoard.

Conclusion

At the end of my work term as a Software Engineer Co-op at ApplyBoard, I felt accomplished in what I had achieved. I had attained a lot of knowledge and skills while working on the Platform team at ApplyBoard. During my 4-month work term at ApplyBoard, I had made important connections with co-workers and expanded my professional network. To conclude, I had a great experience at ApplyBoard and look forward to returning as a co-op again in Winter 2021.
